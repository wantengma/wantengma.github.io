About Me

I am a PhD student in Statistics at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. I am fortunate to work with Prof. T. Tony Cai. Previously, I obtained a MPhil in Mathematics from HKUST, advised by Prof. Dong Xia. I received my B.S. in Statistics (with honors) and B.A. in Management Information Systems from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in high-dimensional statistics and online learning. Typically, my research focuses on:

  • High-dimensional inference

  • Online decision making

  • Optimization
