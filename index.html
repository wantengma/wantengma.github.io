About Me

I am a graduate student in Mathematics at HKUST. I am very glad to be advised by Prof. Dong Xia. I received my B.S. in Statistics and B.A. in Management Information Systems (MIS) from Chu Kochen honors College, Zhejiang University. I also work closely with Prof. Lilun Du (City University of Hong Kong), Prof. Ming Yuan (Columbia University), and Prof. Jiashuo Jiang (HKUST).

News

Starting from this fall, I will begin my Ph.D. studies in Statistics at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania.

Research

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in high-dimensional statistics and online learning. Typically, my research focuses on:

  • High-dimensional Inference

  • Online Allocation

  • Optimization

  • Multiple Testing
